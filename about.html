About Baam Tara PG

Welcome to Baam Tara PG, a trusted and student-friendly Paying Guest accommodation. Our goal is to provide a clean, secure, and comfortable living environment for students and professionals.

We offer various types of rooms including AC and Non-AC options, 2-bed and 3-bed sharing rooms, as well as single rooms with attached bathrooms and kitchen access. Our rooftop and hall area provide space for relaxation and social gatherings.

Amenities include free Wi-Fi, regular room cleaning, washing machines, refrigerators, and 24/7 water availability. Our kitchen serves nutritious meals throughout the day.

Whether you're a student or a working professional, Baam Tara PG ensures a homely atmosphere with modern facilities, all at an affordable rent.
